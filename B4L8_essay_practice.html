Part 2 of 4 · Second Paragraph (1/2)
Reasons
原因——為什麼會這樣？
📖 示範句 — 直接參考，不用自己寫這段
There are several reasons why pets have taken on such an important role in people's lives. To begin with, as Taiwan's birth rate continues to decline, many couples choose to raise pets instead of having children, treating them as substitutes for family members. Furthermore, in an increasingly fast-paced and stressful society, pets provide emotional comfort and companionship that many people find difficult to obtain elsewhere. In addition, the rise of social media has made it easier for pet owners to share their experiences, which has further promoted the culture of pet ownership among younger generations.
