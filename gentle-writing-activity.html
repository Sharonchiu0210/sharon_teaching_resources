Gentle Encouragement Writing

How to sound supportive — and a little poetic.
What is this style?

Some writing does not try to sound powerful at the beginning.

Instead, it first makes the reader feel understood.

Then it gently changes the way we see the situation — without blaming anyone. At the end, it offers just one small step.

Common Sentence Patterns
You are not ______. You are just ______.
You may think ______, but actually ______.
Because ______, we can ______.
So today, just ______.
Writing Recipe
  • Notice a feeling.
  • Do not blame the person.
  • Change the way they see it.
  • Give one small step.
  • End with warmth.
Example
You are not bad at English.
You are just meeting some words for the first few times.

You may think reading a word once is not enough.
But actually, every time you look at it again,
it becomes a little more familiar.

So today, do not try to remember everything.
Just walk one small step forward.

Choose a topic, then fill in each step. Your paragraph will come together at the bottom.
